About Us

Rapid Route helps commuters stay on time with accurate, real-time bus tracking you can depend on every day.

Our Story

I started Rapid Route after dealing with late buses, and inaccurate tracking apps during daily commutes. I wanted a simpler and more reliable way for students and workers to know exactly when their bus would arrive. This is how my buisness came about.

Rapid Route was built for people who rely on public transportation every day and need information they can trust.

What We Stand For

Accuracy

We focus on delivering real-time tracking updates commuters can rely on.

Reliability

Our goal is to reduce missed buses and help riders stay on schedule every day.

Simplicity

Rapid Route is designed to be clean, fast, and easy to use for all commuters.

As a daily bus rider, I experienced the frustration of unreliable bus schedules and inaccurate timing. That experience inspired me to create Rapid Route.

My goal is to make commuting easier for students, workers, and anyone who depends on public transportation every day.
